Teen Build Up

Teen Build Up is a free online platform designed to help boost
start-ups, clubs, and events created by middle and high schoolers.



Learn More

Advertise Your Start-Up, Club, or Event

As technology expands, an increasing number of teenagers are starting businesses and need a way to spread the word to the relevant market. Teen Build-Up is providing a free online platform for high school and middle school start-up and club owners to advertise their services and gain followers.




Get A Boost

Other than just for getting views and spreading the word, start-up owners and event hosts can use Teen Build Up as a means to attain volunteers for service work, orders for products, interns, donations, etc.



Join the Community

Join a community of other teen entrepreneurs and problem-solvers and stay tuned with what is new. Join services and charities that stand for what you believe in.



Find Volunteering and Interning Opportunities

Find volunteering and interning opportunities in other start-ups based on your interests and make your resume stand out.



Teen Build Up Connect Platform

As a business, use the Teen Build Up Connect Platform to interact with your direct audience, post recent events, and send updates like meeting times.
